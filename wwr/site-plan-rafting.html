Overview

Purpose

Here at Idaho White Water Rafting we are here to bring the best of rafting to you. We are designed to take youon the most exciting thrill seeking adventure of your life. Located in Northern Idaho we know how to make this experience one you will never forget. Whether you are a first time rafter or and experienced enthusiest we have it all.

Audience

Has life been a bit slow for you lately? Do you want to get in touch with the outdoors? Are you looking for a thrill? Then you are looking for a new White Water Rafting trip! We cater to all types of people. Whether you are a first time rider or a seasoned vet, we have the adventure you've been looking for.
